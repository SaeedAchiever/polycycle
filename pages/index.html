Our Story

Sankofa: Go Back and Fetch It

"Sankofa" — an Akan word meaning it is not wrong to go back for what you forgot. We believe the plastic we discarded is not waste, but a resource we forgot.

EcoSankofa was born at the intersection of environmental crisis and community resilience. Working in the Agbogbloshie area and beyond, we train youth to collect, sort, and craft plastic waste into premium-quality products sold globally.

Every product you purchase directly funds training, wages, and dignity for the people making them.
